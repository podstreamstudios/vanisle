VanIsle

A regional system for developing culture, access, and economic value on Vancouver Island.

VanIsle is a coordinated set of initiatives designed to develop Vancouver Island as a connected system — linking cultural activity, visitor access, and export-ready products.

The Opportunity

Strong assets. Fragmented systems.

Vancouver Island has the raw ingredients for a connected regional economy. They just aren't wired together yet.

Assets
  • Strong cultural communities
  • High tourism demand
  • Premium craft production
  • Proximity to major markets such as Vancouver
Gaps
  • Cultural activity is fragmented
  • Access is inconsistent without a vehicle
  • Export pathways are underdeveloped
The System

Three coordinated layers

VanIsle operates across culture, access, and craft — each layer reinforcing the others.

Cultural Layer
Megaband
Vancouver Island Region

A large-scale, coordinated performance system that connects musicians and communities across the region.

  • Builds shared cultural visibility
  • Engages participants and audiences
  • Creates region-wide presence
Access Layer
IslandCamp
Car-free travel system

A car-free travel and equipment system enabling visitors to explore Vancouver Island without bringing a vehicle.

  • Reduces travel friction
  • Enables mobility across the region
  • Connects arrival points to destinations
Product Layer
VanIsle Spirits
VICSS

An export-focused initiative positioning Vancouver Island craft spirits in global markets.

  • Develops international demand
  • Supports regional producers
  • Builds economic value
How It Works

Culture → Access → Value

Experimental Layer

System compositions

VanIsle layers are composable. Combining them produces new experiential and economic architectures.

IslandCamp × VICSS

Car-free camping experiences that embed craft distillery visits, converting place-based experience into export-ready goods.

IslandCamp × Megaband

Camping systems integrated with distributed cultural performances across Vancouver Island.

Regional Context

Vancouver Island, British Columbia

Grounded in Vancouver Island with proximity to Vancouver and access to international markets.

Innovation Layer

Developed in collaboration with 1730.ca — applying coordination across independent systems, data-informed targeting and development, and integration of cultural, logistical, and economic layers.

Research Context

Systems design & cybernetics

Developed within a systems design and cybernetics research framework — exploring requisite variety, coordination across independent systems, and place-based experiential economies.

Expected Outcomes

What this produces

  • Increased regional visibility and participation
  • Improved visitor access without vehicle dependency
  • Growth in export-ready products and international markets
  • A scalable model for regional development
